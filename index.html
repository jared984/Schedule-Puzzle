AeroScheduler Rules

Welcome to AeroScheduler. This simulation tests your logistical efficiency, timeline logic, and spatial coordination.

Line Swapping

Drag any day cell up or down to swap. Dragging also swaps every day after.

Schedule Conflicts

Aircraft cannot depart less than 14 hours before landing the day prior. Conflicts cost -2,000 points.

Auto-Repo Flights

Planes landing at a different airport from their next origin trigger a blue Repo. Flight time costs points.

Speed Bonus

Submitting rewards +10 points per remaining second on the clock.

Goal: Drag and arrange the flights so that all aircraft schedules are conflict-free, and total repositioning flight time is kept as low as possible.
